McJoski Enterprise Corporation is a company that provides logistics solution such as transportation of goods and hauling of materials. Its office is located at Brgy. Canlubang, Calamba City.


McJoski Enterprise Corporation was founded by Mark Henry Endaya, a graduate of B.S. Entrepreneurship at Mapua-Malayan Colleges Laguna. He established the company in 2018 after gaining many years of experience in managing multiple businesses of his family. The name of the company was named after his brother, Josh, who passed away in 2015.


The company started with only two employees and eventually grew to 12 employees. The company has served multiple FMCG and logistics companies around Calamba City and they have now expanded to other areas such as Cabuyao City and Batangas City. In 2022 they expanded their services and added hauling of construction materials.
